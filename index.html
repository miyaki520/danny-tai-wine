About Me

Expert in Burgundy · Passionate About Wine

In 2023, Danny Tai celebrates his 11th year in the food and beverage industry. Despite his extensive experience, he remains committed to the never-ending journey of learning about wine. Aligning with the Institute of Masters of Wine’s motto, “Scientia Atque Integritas” (Knowledge and Integrity), Danny embodies this principle through his dedication to the craft.

Danny’s profound passion for wine, particularly the fine wines of Burgundy, is evident in his approach. He perceives the grand vins de Bourgogne as a masterpiece—a harmonious blend of earth, climate, and human artistry developed over two millennia. As the Burgundy region evolves towards greater refinement and sustainability, so does Danny’s expertise in viticulture and winemaking.

Driven by a sense of duty, Danny is dedicated to sharing, guiding, and fostering a deeper appreciation for Burgundy wines. He views this mission as his life's purpose, striving to elevate others' understanding and enjoyment of these exceptional wines.

02 Achievements & Portfolios

Enchanting the Shines

Pioneering Malaysian Excellence in the Wine World

Danny’s journey in the wine industry is a true breakthrough story, inspiring and motivational. Without overseas experience, Danny has risen to international acclaim, showcasing the power of local talent. Achieving the prestigious Level 4 Diploma from the Wine & Spirit Education Trust (WSET) in London, Danny has reached a level of recognition rarely awarded, especially for those without global training. This elite certification underscores his exceptional skills and dedication, positioning him as a trailblazer for Malaysian sommeliers.

Danny’s story is proof that passion and hard work can break any boundary. His achievements not only elevate Malaysia’s presence on the world wine stage but also serve as a beacon of inspiration for aspiring sommeliers across the nation.
